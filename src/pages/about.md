Music Reviews is a home for long-form album reviews — honest, detailed writing about records worth your time.

## How we rate

Every review on this site is a considered take on an album's place in its genre and the broader musical conversation. We rate on a 1–10 scale, with no half-points and no grade inflation.

| Rating | Label | What it means |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| **10** | Essential | A landmark record. If you care about music, you need to hear this. |
| **8–9** | Great | Excellent — stands out in its genre and rewards repeat listens. |
| **6–7** | Good | Solid work with clear strengths, even if it doesn't reinvent the wheel. |
| **4–5** | Below Average | Has moments, but serious weaknesses hold it back. |
| **1–3** | Bad | Fails at what it sets out to do. Not recommended. |

Reviews are written in Markdown and managed through the CMS at [/admin](/admin). If you're a reviewer, log in there to draft and publish new reviews.
